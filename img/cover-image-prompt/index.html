    Cover Image Prompt¶

    Design Parameters¶

    • Title: Food Science for 9th Grade
    • Keywords: food, science, water, school, identify, acid, explain, nutrient, project, yeast
    • Motifs: acid, explain, nutrient, project, yeast, starter, temperature
    • Color Palette: deep blue, teal, white, subtle orange accent
    • Composition: Centered title with abstract/montage background
    • Size: 1200×630 pixels (1.91:1 aspect ratio)
    • Output file: docs/img/cover.png

    Image Prompt¶

    Copy and paste into ChatGPT, DALL-E, Midjourney, or another AI image generator:

    Please generate a new 1200x630 pixels (1.91:1 aspect ratio) image. 

Create a modern, professional wide-landscape book cover for 'Food Science for 9th Grade'.

The image has three core components.

LEFT 25%: Image of the "welcome" pose for the mascot Zyme 
RIGHT 75%: Title in the center
BACKGROUND: Montage of images from the food science concepts

The title 'Food Science' should be prominently displayed in large, crisp, high-contrast white or light text, centered on the image. 

The background should feature a sophisticated montage or abstract visualization related to:  vegetables, bread, science, water (H2O), acid (ph), stove-top wok, smell of garlic, garden with tomatoes, nutrients (vitamins), restaurants, fast food, supply chain, packaged goods, yeast. 

Use a modern color palette with deep blues, teals, and subtle accent colors. The design should be clean, professional, and not cluttered. No other text besides the title. No logos, watermarks, or trademarks. Style: modern textbook cover, professional, educational, high-quality digital art.

    Instructions¶

    1. Copy the IMAGE PROMPT above
    2. Paste it into ChatGPT Plus (or another AI image generator)
    3. Download the generated image
    4. Save it to: docs/img/cover.png
    5. Resize to 1200×630 pixels if needed
